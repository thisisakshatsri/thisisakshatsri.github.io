Life is Weird

28 April . 6 min read

Life is WEIRD.

You make connections and you know that he or she might leave because there is no other way. You Accept. You'll no longer than aquantainces. Sounds weird?

You cry because you are sick and in consequence you can’t even eat or can’t go for vacations with your friends but there are people who are hungry for days and can’t afford food because destiny made them poor. So who will cry for them? Life is weird.

You are depressed because few months back someone close to you had left and you constantly thinking about it. But few months back Life was pretty normal but now Life...is...just...weird.
